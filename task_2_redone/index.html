INFO

Deltarune is a role-playing game (RPG) created by Toby Fox, the developer behind Undertale. It takes place in a world separate from Undertale but shares some characters and themes.

PLOT

The game follows Kris, a human, and their new friend Susie, as they are pulled into the Dark World, a mysterious realm ruled by the Dark Fountain. There, they meet Ralsei, a kind prince who claims to be a "Darkner" destined to maintain balance.

GAMEPLAY

It is a turn-based combat with bullet-hell dodging mechanics that is similar to Undertale.

Puzzles, exploration, and interactions with quirky characters.

You can win battles by sparing or fighting your enemies.

STATUS

The game is currently not complete and only 4 out of 7 chapters are available.
